Web Banners

Web banners are one of the most prolific forms of marketing used in the digital media. With my designs I intend to “WOW” people for the right reasons. The purpose of having a banner ad is to attract traffic to a website by clicking on the ad. I make banners in different formats: simple text an image (GIF, JPEG, PNG), Shockwave or Flash, animation, sound, or video to maximize presence. I have created banners for clients from different fields like retail, e-commerce, real estate, education, entertainment, individual websites, corporate websites, flash websites etc. With our advertising partners, I can also create an online advertising strategy for your display ads to reach your ideal customer.This further refines your target audience and increases your return on investment.
