Commercial Banking

Commercial Banking is focused on helping our clients succeed and making a positive difference in our communities. We provide credit and financing, treasury and payment services, international banking and real estate services to clients including corporations, municipalities, institutions, real estate investors and owners, and nonprofit organizations

My work

As a UI designer for Commercial Banking, I designed the user interface screens based on the requirements gathered from stakeholders.

Here are some of the key screens that I designed:

  • Credit and Financing Screen: Clients can apply for loans and credit lines, and view their credit history and repayment terms. The UI screens may also include calculators for estimating loan payments and interest rates. It should also provide quick access to frequently used features and reports.
  • Treasury and Payment Services Screen: Clients can manage their cash flow and make payments using a variety of methods, such as wire transfers, ACH payments, and online bill pay. The UI screens may include a dashboard for viewing cash balances and transaction history, and may also provide alerts for incoming and outgoing payments.
  • International Banking screen: Clients can manage their international transactions, such as foreign currency exchanges and wire transfers, through the platform. The UI screens may include real-time exchange rates and market data, and may also provide resources for navigating international trade and regulatory requirements.
  • Real Estate Services Screen: Clients can access real estate investment and management services, such as property management, leasing, and financing. The UI screens may include property listings, market data, and financial analysis tools for evaluating potential investments.
  • Nonprofit Services Screen: Clients can access specialized services for nonprofit organizations, such as grant management, fundraising, and donor management. The UI screens may include resources for compliance and regulatory requirements, and may also provide networking opportunities with other nonprofit organizations.

User Research

When conducting UX research on an Commercial Banking Software, there are several key points of concern that should be considered:

Mobile optimization: Many users access recruitment tools on mobile devices

Competitor analysis: what other recruitment tools are available in the market

Technical constraints:limited functionality or need to integrate with existing systems

Balance competing interests: while still prioritizing the needs of the users

Create user journey maps:to improve the user experience.
