It's me, Achyutha -
and it's nice to see you here!

Born and raised Indian, I am currently living in Chicago, Illinois. I graduated from Missouri State University with a Masters degree in Computer Science.

My path into UX/UI began at a small startup. I learned to conduct user research, created workflows and designs that helped define a new MVP product and established a collaborative design system.

I am a curious creative who likes to explore and learn about what could be and a down-to-earth critical thinker who likes to evaluate what works best for now and the steps needed to move forward.

Through my work, I want to help create useful, engaging, and intuitive solutions that help people improve their lives.

What I do when I'm not working,
I love to travel and explore new places – to immerse myself in various cultures.
I have a personal goal of tasting all the street food in every place I visit. I believe street food gives me better cultural insight than any history books. This is the one goal I hope to never achieve.
